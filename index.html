Robotics@UH Onboarding Program

Build on the engineering and technical skills you already have to gain the robotics foundation, confidence, and credibility to collaborate across teams, move into hardware-focused roles, and lead technical innovation at the Cullen College of Engineering.

Robotics@UH Onboarding Program Highlights

Hands-on, in-person labs – Meet weekly with engineering leads for interactive sessions focused on real robot behavior, motion control, and hardware integration.

Advanced engineering projects – Learn to build robotic systems that are precise, responsive, safe, and effective using STM32 architectures and custom PCBs.

Applied autonomy foundations – Build an understanding of how systems move, interact, and make decisions through hands-on exploration of ROS2 programming and perception.

The Cullen College advantage – Collaborate with top-tier student engineers to prepare for regional competitions and industry showcases.

Lab Grants & Funding

Submit your project proposal by the priority deadline to receive partial funding awards for custom parts and fabrication.
