Software & Systems Architecture

Our software teams write the intelligence that bridges our hardware to the real world.

Embedded Firmware

We write bare-metal C/C++ to interface directly with STM32 microcontrollers, ensuring high-frequency sensor polling and immediate actuator response times.

ROS2 Integration

Utilizing a node-based communication protocol, we handle advanced perception data, motion planning, and autonomous decision-making for our rovers and UAVs.

Simulation & Silicon Photonics

To optimize our systems, we run extensive multi-physics simulations. Our advanced research teams also explore KLayout software design and silicon photonics concepts for next-generation energy harvesting and grid architecture.
