Active Hardware & Platforms

Our mechanical and electrical engineering divisions collaborate to design and fabricate custom autonomous systems.

Line-of-Sight Micro UAV

A custom-built quadcopter platform designed for rapid deployment. Integrated with an STM32 flight controller and custom sensor fusion boards to maintain extreme stability.

All-Terrain Robotic Rover

A heavy-duty skid-steer chassis built for rough environmental navigation. It utilizes precise encoder feedback loops paired with LiDAR integration for mapping and obstacle avoidance.

4-DOF Robotic Manipulator

A precision pick-and-place automation system leveraging 4-axis inverse kinematics, servo/stepper actuation, and custom-machined gripper jaw mechanics.
