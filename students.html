Student Resources & Lab Access

Information for active Cullen College engineering students and Robotics@UH members.

Lab Access Hours

The main fabrication lab is open to certified members from Monday to Friday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Weekend access requires a faculty sponsor present.

Equipment Checkout

Members can check out soldering kits, multimeters, and temporary microcontrollers using the official lab inventory portal. Items must be returned within 48 hours.
